01 — Profile

At the intersection
of trust & story.

I'm a senior communications and editorial leader with more than a decade of experience directing global content operations and managing distributed teams in high-stakes environments. My work sits at the intersection of global health, public trust, and institutional storytelling.

At Feature Story News, I led editorial operations for Channel NewsAsia's digital platform — growing monthly unique visitors from 10 to 12 million in a single year, while overseeing human-in-the-loop review of AI-assisted content, editorial standards, and crisis communications.

Earlier, I worked within philanthropy at the Surdna Foundation, supporting $4.1 million in grantmaking across education, economic justice, and community development — giving me a grounded understanding of how communications functions inside mission-driven institutions.

I hold an M.A. in International Educational Development from Columbia University, Teachers College, and a B.A. from Pitzer College.
